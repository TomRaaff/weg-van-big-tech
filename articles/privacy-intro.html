Intro

Privacy is a process. How far do you want to go? In every data processing step, there are multiple parties involved. You can start with making private your part and demand that the other parties do their part as well. How much legislation is there to help you achieve that? Not much unfortunatelly. Present internet is built around the idea that behavior of users can be monitored, analyzed and monetized in exchange for "free" services. If you don't pay for a service, then you're the product being traded. That's what you consent with by accepting tracking and 3rd party cookies in your browsers. Most of the apps you install on your phone do this automatically under the hood.
